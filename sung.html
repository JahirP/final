Biography

Kang's first significant part was as the distant gang member Han Lue in Justin Lin's Better Luck Tomorrow (2002). He played Sam Kim in the film The Motel, where he was one of the stars.In Lin's second film, The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift, Kang returned to the character of Han Lue from the Fast & Furious franchise.
